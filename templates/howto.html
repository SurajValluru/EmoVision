Tutorial

Tap on Camera Feed to know emotion of the person. Camera can be changed in Configurations. The responses are of Four Types:
  • Speak
  • Universal
  • Custom
  • Morse

Speak

Speak response gives audio clip with the current emotion in English on default. Language can be changed in Configurations.

Universal

Universal response gives audio clip with sound of that emotion, which can be understood by everyone without language constraint.

Custom

Custom response gives vibration feedback to mobile. This custom vibrations can be changed in Configurations. Default custom codes are:
  • angry: "..."
  • disgust: "......."
  • fear: "......"
  • happy: "."
  • multi: "-.-"
  • neutral: ".."
  • no: "--"
  • sad: "....."
  • surprise: "...."
Custom Action Terminology:
  • ' . ' for short vibrations
  • ' - ' for long vibrations
  • ' ' for no vibration vibrations

Morse

Morse response gives vibration feedback to mobile with morse code.
